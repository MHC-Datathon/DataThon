BUS SPEED ANALYSIS

Description

Bus speed data generally has info for all buses (except with M14+), so we queried the bus speeds from the MTA’s datasets. We queried by selecting time stamps from 6/20/2025 12AM - 9/10/2025 11:45PM and 6/20/2024 12AM - 9/10/2024 11:45PM respectfully. Then setting Route ID to one of: [M15+, M14+, M23+, M34+, M101, M2, M42, M4], (routes in MCBD with ACE implemented as of currently). Then setting the hour of the day from 5-9 and 14-18 (rush hour) and setting the day of the weeks to be from Monday-Friday.

WHAT WE SEE

The charts are seperated by route type (Limited, Local, SBS). We notice that alongside the decrease of violations, we also tend to see a slight increase in bus speed's. We also notice that every single bus route has seen a increase in average speed post congestion.

KEY FINDINGS

VIOLATIONS DECREASED

Violations decreased alongside congestion pricing in CBD

BUS SPEEDS INCREASED

Bus speeds increased in CBD post-implementation

RIDERSHIP INCREASED

Bus ridership increased significantly in CBD

CONCLUSION

Congestion pricing has had a positive impact on NYC bus operations in the Manhattan Central Business District, resulting in fewer violations, increased speeds, and higher ridership.
